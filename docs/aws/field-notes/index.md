# Field Notes

For small software teams, scattered logs aren't just a hassle—they're a compliance nightmare waiting to happen.
Field Notes gives small businesses professional logging tools without the burden of enterprise overhead.

---

## Why Field Notes?

- **Self-hosted simplicity** — Runs as a lightweight Docker container, so you always control your data.
- **Clean dashboard** — Easy visibility into logs with a straightforward UI.
- **Fast queries** — Quickly search and filter logs for debugging.
- **Compliance-ready** — Upgrade when you need tamper-proof storage and audit-grade features.

---

## How It Works

1. **Deploy Field Notes** as a Docker container inside your environment.
2. **Collect logs** from your applications and services.
3. **Query and debug** using the built-in dashboard.
4. **Scale up** with advanced compliance features when your team is ready.
