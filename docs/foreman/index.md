# Overview: Foreman

Choosing an orchestrator often forces you to compromise. Many tools are Python-first, leaving your Go or C# teams feeling like second-class citizens. Worse, many cloud platforms require you to upload your proprietary code and data just to run a workflow.

Foreman is the language-agnostic orchestrator that respects your stack and your security. It's a full-featured workflow engine that runs in your environment. You configure your DAGs, triggers, and container events from our clean dashboard, but your code and data never leave your control.
